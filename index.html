Academic Projects

Anamolous Sound Detection using Supervised and Unsupervised Machine Learning Techniques

We developed two deep learning architectures to detect malfunctions in industrial machines using the MIMII dataset. We created a supervised CNN Model that was trained using cross-entropy loss and evaluated its performance using ROC AUC and an unsupervised model with a pre-trained ResNet18 model and a fine tuned GMM for anomaly detection.
Ryan Roby, Min-Gyu Kim, Adhika Retnanto, Sophia Shi
Dec 2023
PDF

MEMS based Peizoelectric Vibrational Energy Harvestor

We designed a piezo electric energy harvester that harnesses the vibrations resulting from the impact of rainwater on a flexible cantilever to generate power. We simulated experiments using COMSOL to determine the effects of droplet size and impact velocity on peak voltage generated by the energy harvester. We also verified the results in the lab using a proof of concept model.
Ryan Roby, Krishnadev R Menon
